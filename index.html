Welcome to my page! My name is Shalini (SHAH-lynn-ee) Nair and my preferred pronouns are she/her/hers.

I'm an soon-to-be graduate who is passionate about developing data-informed solutions to emerging public health issues. I have demonstrated knowledge in a variety of settings ranging from non-communicable diseases and clinical research to pandemic response.

I have developed a background in project management, data analysis in SAS and R, and have an invested interest in infectious diseases and public health preparedness.

I have a Bachelor of Public Health and am on track to complete my Master of Public Health in May 2021 - both from the University of Florida (Go Gators!)

  • Website: shalininair.com (You are here!)

  • Degree: Master of Public Health (In Progress)
  • Email: shalsnair11@gmail.com

Currently, I am completing my final internship experience with the Public Health and Medical Preparedness Unit at the Florida Department of Health in Monroe County assisting with the roll-out of COVID-19 vaccinations.

I'm a firm believer that learning is a lifelong process. In my free time, I enjoy coding, exercising, and furthering my knowledge. Follow along on my #SelfInvestmentSundays on LinkedIn to see what I'm learning every week!
