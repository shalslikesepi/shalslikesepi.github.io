About

Learn more about me

Senior Public Health Analyst


My name is Shalini Nair (SHAH-lynn-ee; she/her/hers). I am a highly motivated and results-driven program analyst working on national level public health issues, with an ultimate interest in helping advance progressive changes to systems-level health and social policies.

I am a certified public health professional with 6+ years of experience in public health operations, evidence-based research, and health policy. I have a proven track record of managing multiple priorities across $6,000,000+ project portfolios, fostering strong collaborative partnerships across divisions and stakeholders, and distilling complex insights into impactful content for diverse audiences. I am passionate about translating evidence-based practice into actionable policy recommendations and improved health outcomes.

I hold both a Bachelor's and a Master's in Public Health—with a concentration in epidemiology, from the University of Florida (Go Gators!) and am currently based in Washington, D.C.

As a firm believer that learning is a lifelong process, I enjoy coding (this website), exercising, and furthering my knowledge. Follow along on LinkedIn to see what I'm up to!
